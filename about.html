Our story

A little shop with a big heart.

Nana's Recyclables started as a love letter to the neighborhood — a place where good things get a second chance and folks find treasures they didn't know they needed. Tucked inside the historic Moody Building on Crescent Street, the shop has become a little gathering spot for collectors, browsers, and the kindly curious.

You'll find dishes, lamps, jewelry, books, movies, vinyl, furniture, and the occasional oddity — all picked with care and priced to find a new home. Stop in, chat a while, and see what's new on the shelves.

"A little bit of everything with a warm and friendly lady at the helm." — Fran Parker, Google review

We're proud to be part of the Claremont community, and grateful for every neighbor who walks through the door. Thanks for keeping our little shop going.
